Recipes - World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adopted this recipe from a book caled Sydney Food by Bil Granger.Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Ba's restaurant in king croos.
Sydney.I have been after the recipe I have since transferred it into what i really believe are thye best scramled eggs i have ever tasted.

This recipe is what i call a "very special breakfast",just look at the ingredients to see why.It has to be tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly miled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs,cream and salt in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat(taking care not to burn the butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan.
  4. Using a wooden spatuls,bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet.and let it cook for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold contents in again,leave for 20 seconds,and repeat until the eggs are only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly miled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
